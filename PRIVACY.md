# Privacy Policy for Organic Tab Sorter

Last Updated: January 17, 2026

## Data Collection and Storage

Organic Tab Sorter respects your privacy and minimizes data collection:

### What We Store

- **Default Sort Preference**: The extension stores your preferred sort mode (title or URL) using Chrome's sync storage. This allows your preference to sync across your Chrome browsers when signed in to your Google account.

### What We DON'T Collect

- We do NOT collect, transmit, or share any of your browsing data
- We do NOT track which websites you visit
- We do NOT access the content of your web pages
- We do NOT collect any personally identifiable information
- We do NOT send any data to external servers
- We do NOT use analytics or tracking tools

## Permissions Explained

The extension requests the following permissions:

- **tabs**: Required to read tab titles and URLs for sorting, and to reorder tabs in the current window
- **storage**: Required to save your default sort preference locally in your browser
- **tabGroups**: Required to detect and preserve tab groups during sorting operations

## Data Security

All data is stored locally in your Chrome browser using Chrome's built-in storage API. Your sort preference syncs through Google's secure Chrome Sync service if you're signed in to Chrome.

## Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party services, analytics, or external APIs.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the extension's repository and the "Last Updated" date will be modified.

## Contact

For questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the extension's GitHub repository.
